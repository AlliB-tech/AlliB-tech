## Scoring Guide

**Impact:** Low / Medium / High  
**Likelihood:** Low / Medium / High  
**Risk Rating:** Low / Medium / High based on business impact and probability  

## Notes

This template is designed for a lightweight GRC program. It can be expanded with numerical scoring, control IDs, mapped frameworks, remediation deadlines, and executive reporting fields.
