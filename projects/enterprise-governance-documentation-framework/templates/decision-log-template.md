# Decision Log Template

## Purpose

A decision log preserves the reasoning behind important choices so teams can understand what was decided, why it was decided, who approved it, and what evidence supported the decision.

## Decision Quality Standard

A strong decision should include:

- Clear business reason
- Options considered
- Evidence reviewed
- Owner or approver
- Expected impact
- Follow-up action
- Status or closure criteria

## Principle

Unrecorded decisions become organizational memory gaps. A decision log turns judgment into evidence.
