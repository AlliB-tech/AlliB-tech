## Purpose

An evidence register keeps proof organized before an audit, investigation, review, or management request. Evidence should be collected during normal operations, not only after a problem occurs.

## Evidence Quality Standard

Good evidence should be:

- Current
- Complete
- Traceable
- Owned by a responsible person or team
- Stored in a known location
- Linked to a risk, control, policy, process, or requirement
